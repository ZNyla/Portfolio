About Me:

I am currently a student at the Base Camp Coding Academy (BCCA) in Water Valley, MS. I enrolled at BCCA to further explore my passion for coding, film, and writing. My interest in coding began when I started creating interactive stories on an app called Episode. As I grew older and gained more experience, I realized I wanted to combine my love for storytelling and film with technology. My goal is to become a VFX software developer or a virtual reality (VR) developer that allow me to bring playable stories to life through immersive, cutting-edge technology. In addition, I have a growing interest in artificial intelligence (AI), particularly as it continues to impact the creative industries. Rather than seeing AI as a replacement for human creativity, I am passionate about understanding how we can collaborate with AI to enhance our work. I aspire to become a responsible AI ethics specialist to deepen my understanding of ethical AI use and contribute to guiding my industry in integrating AI in thoughtful and responsible ways.
